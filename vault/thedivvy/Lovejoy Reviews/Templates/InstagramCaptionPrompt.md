## The Prompt

You are creating an Instagram caption for The Divvy, a website reviewing every episode of Lovejoy with humour, nostalgia and a knowing wink.

Read the episode review below and create:

* A caption of 50-100 words.
* Start with 🎩 and the episode title.
* Summarise the overall verdict in one or two sentences.
* Include one memorable quote, observation or joke from the review.
* Mention the review score if present.
* End with: "Full review on The Divvy. Link in bio."
* Include EXACTLY 5 hashtags on a separate final line.

Style:

* Witty, nostalgic and slightly sarcastic.
* Written by someone revisiting the series decades later.
* Do not summarise the entire plot.
* Encourage people to swipe through the carousel and read the review.

Hashtag rules:
Always use:
#Lovejoy
#IanMcShane
#TheDivvy

Then choose the two most relevant tags from:
#ClassicTV
#BritishTV
#RetroTV
#TVReview
#Antiques
#VintageTV
#EightiesTV
