## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Series overview information
The series page SHALL provide an overview of the series including title and description.

#### Scenario: User views series overview
- **WHEN** a visitor navigates to a series page
- **THEN** the page displays the series title and an overview description

### Requirement: All episodes clearly listed
The series page SHALL display all episodes in the series with episode cards.

#### Scenario: User browses series episodes
- **WHEN** a visitor views a series page
- **THEN** all episodes display as episode cards showing title, episode number, score, summary, and image if available

### Requirement: Clear episode ordering
The series page SHALL order episodes clearly by episode number.

#### Scenario: Episodes in sequence
- **WHEN** a visitor views a series page
- **THEN** episodes display in sequential order from first to last episode

### Requirement: Optional statistics
The series page MAY display optional statistics such as average score or number of episodes.

#### Scenario: Series statistics displayed
- **WHEN** a visitor views a series page
- **THEN** aggregate information like average Lovejoy Units or total episode count MAY display

### Requirement: Inviting presentation
The series page SHALL feel inviting and curated rather than database-like.

#### Scenario: Page feels curated
- **WHEN** a visitor browses a series page
- **THEN** the design uses The Divvy's visual identity with warm colors, elegant typography, and generous spacing
