## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Hero section communicates site purpose
The homepage SHALL display a hero section that immediately conveys The Divvy is a Lovejoy episode review site.

#### Scenario: First-time visitor understands purpose
- **WHEN** a visitor lands on the homepage
- **THEN** the hero section displays the site name, a clear tagline explaining it's a Lovejoy episode review site, and establishes nostalgic visual identity

### Requirement: Featured review prominently displayed
The homepage SHALL feature the latest or highlighted episode review prominently.

#### Scenario: Latest review is accessible
- **WHEN** a visitor views the homepage
- **THEN** the featured review card displays with episode title, series/episode number, excerpt, score, and image if available

### Requirement: Series navigation available
The homepage SHALL provide clear navigation to browse reviews by series.

#### Scenario: User browses by series
- **WHEN** a visitor wants to explore a specific series
- **THEN** series navigation displays all available series with episode counts and links to series pages

### Requirement: Recent reviews listed
The homepage SHALL display a list of recent episode reviews beyond the featured review.

#### Scenario: User sees latest content
- **WHEN** a visitor scrolls past the hero and featured review
- **THEN** recent reviews display as episode cards showing title, series/episode, score, and summary

### Requirement: Introduction explains concept
The homepage SHALL include a short introduction section explaining The Divvy's unique approach and value proposition.

#### Scenario: User understands site concept
- **WHEN** a visitor reads the homepage introduction
- **THEN** the text clearly explains this is a thoughtful, witty companion guide to Lovejoy with observations, humour, and continuity spotting

### Requirement: Visual personality established
The homepage SHALL establish The Divvy's warm, nostalgic, British, eccentric visual identity.

#### Scenario: Homepage feels distinctive
- **WHEN** a visitor views the homepage
- **THEN** the design uses warm cream/parchment backgrounds, dark green/burgundy/gold accents, elegant serif headings, and antique-inspired aesthetic
